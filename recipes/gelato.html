Creamy Homemade Gelato

This Creamy Homemade Gelato delivers an authentic taste of Italy right from your kitchen. Smoother, denser, and silkier than regular ice cream, this gelato is made with simple, high-quality ingredients that highlight rich, natural flavors.

Unlike traditional ice cream, gelato uses more milk and less cream, giving it a lighter texture without sacrificing creaminess. The slow-churning process creates a dense, velvety consistency that melts slowly in your mouth, intensifying each flavor. Whether you go for a classic vanilla bean, rich chocolate, or fresh fruit varieties like strawberry or lemon, this gelato recipe ensures a luscious and refreshing treat.

Steps

  1. Heat the Milk and Cream
  2. Whisk the Egg Yolks and Sugar
  3. Temper the Eggs
  4. Cook the Custard Base
  5. Strain and Cool the Mixture
  6. Churn the Gelato
  7. Let it Rest
  8. Freeze and Serve
