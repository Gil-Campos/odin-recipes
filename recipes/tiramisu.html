Classic Italian Tiramisu

This Classic Italian Tiramisu is an elegant, creamy dessert that’s both light and indulgent. Made with layers of delicate ladyfinger biscuits dipped in rich espresso and layered with a smooth, velvety mascarpone cream, it’s the perfect balance of sweetness and a subtle coffee kick.

The mascarpone filling is gently folded with whipped eggs and sugar, creating a light, airy texture that contrasts beautifully with the soaked ladyfingers. A generous dusting of high-quality cocoa powder on top adds just the right hint of bitterness, making every bite melt in your mouth.

Tiramisu is a crowd-pleaser that’s surprisingly easy to make, requiring no baking and just a few simple ingredients. Whether you’re serving it at a dinner party or enjoying a quiet treat at home, this classic recipe brings a little taste of Italy to your table.

Steps

  1. Prepare the Espresso
  2. Separate the Eggs
  3. Make the Mascarpone Cream
  4. Whip the Egg Whites
  5. Dip the Ladyfingers
  6. Assemble the Tiramisu
  7. Dust with Cocoa
  8. Chill the Tiramisu
  9. Serve and Enjoy!
